The Cove76 Difference

Detox removes the substance.
Rehab stabilises the person.
Cove76 fixes the pain.

Addiction is the solution to pain. Until you deal with the pain underneath, nothing sticks. That's what makes Cove76 different — we're not managing a condition, we're building a person from the inside out.

Every Person Is Cherished

This is not a bed-rental facility. Every resident is seen as a whole person, with a story, a potential, and a future worth fighting for. VIP service means your loved one is never just a number.

Cove76 is a Self-Esteem Machine

A massive emphasis is placed on rebuilding self-worth from the ground up. Lasting recovery doesn't start with rules. It starts with a person believing they deserve a better life.

Education, Career & Purpose

We help residents find jobs, pursue education, and discover what lights them up. Sobriety without direction isn't recovery. It's waiting. Cove76 gives life something to move toward.

Whole-Person Health

Mental health, physical health, exercise, nutrition, therapy, counseling, creativity, everything a person at this stage of life genuinely needs. Not just staying clean, but becoming well.

Connecting to Hashem

Prayer, davening, Torah, these aren't extras at Cove76, they're the backbone. For many residents, reconnecting with Hashem is the moment everything else starts to hold.

Connection Is the Cure

The opposite of addiction is connection. Alumni come back for Shabbos, for simchas, to mentor the next person in. When people choose to return after moving on, that says everything.

Common Questions

Things families ask us first.

No. Cove76 is a sober living residence, a supportive home environment for people in recovery. We are not a licensed clinical treatment facility and do not provide medical or psychiatric services directly. Residents work with their own therapists, doctors, and support teams, and we coordinate with those professionals when appropriate.
It means the home is fully kosher, mehadrin kitchen, Shabbos observed, and daily life structured around Jewish values. Residents don't need to be strictly observant, but they live in a household that is, and many find that rhythm grounding in a way they hadn't expected.
Reach out through our contact page or call Joe directly. We'll schedule a confidential fit call, usually within 24 hours, to discuss the individual's situation and whether Cove76 is the right match. There's no pressure, no commitment, and no judgment in that first conversation.
Family communication is something we take seriously, and carefully. We establish clear communication agreements with residents and families so everyone knows what to expect. Our goal is to keep families engaged in a healthy way while protecting the privacy and dignity of each resident.
